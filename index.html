The 2004 Indonesian Tsunami By Max and Ibs

The 2004 Indonesian Tsunami was an event that cause major tragedy, including 230,000 deaths. This occured on december 26 2004 and lasted 7 hours.

An earthquake with a magnitude of 9.1 caused the tsunami to come rushing towards the coast of sumatra. This tsunami was not predicted and caught them all by surprise.

luckily indonesia recieved good support from other countries of 13.5 million USD and were almost recovered in 5 years. The tsunami traveled as fast as 500 mph!
